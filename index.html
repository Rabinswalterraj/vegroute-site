Quality is Our Priority

What we do?

  • (1) Procurement & Quality Checks: Our field procurement executives will run quality checks of the fresh produce from the farms.
  • (2) Farms to Fulfillment Centers: The fresh produce which is procured is then sent to the nearby fulfillment centers.
  • (3) Quality Packing: By quick analysis, knowing the characteristics of fresh produce, it's all safely and hygienically packed in different packing materials.
  • (4) Last Mile Delivery: Our last mile delivery teams are assigned to deliver the orders to customers within the next few hours of ordering.
